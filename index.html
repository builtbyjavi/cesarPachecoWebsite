Meet Cesar

Cesar Pacheco helps real estate developers find suitable development sites and investors invest in real estate assets that meet their investment criteria. He is an agent at Keller Williams Advisors and also works part time for a small family office focusing on multifamily investments. Cesar grew up in Fresno, California. He holds a degree from the University of California, Los Angeles and is also an alumni of the USC Ross Minority Program in Real Estate. He is the current president of the UCLA Latino Alumni Association, a board member of Bruin Professionals - UCLA's premier networking organization, and member of the UCLA Real Estate Alumni Group.
